Choose a difficulty to begin.

How to Play

The dealer draws one card at a time.

Place each card into one of the three rows:

  • God Loved
  • He Gave
  • We Believe

Each row must add up exactly to 16. When a row reaches 16, it locks. Complete all three rows to win and reveal John 3:16.

You may discard cards into the Temptation pile, but be careful:

  • Easy Mode: Temptation limit is 12
  • Medium Mode: Temptation limit is 8
  • Hard Mode: Temptation limit is 5

If Temptation reaches the limit, you lose.

Grace Tokens save you when a row goes over 16. If you bust a row and have a Grace Token, the card that caused the bust is removed and 1 Grace Token is used.

A Grace Card gives you +1 Grace Token when drawn.

You lose if:

  • The deck runs out
  • Temptation reaches the limit
  • You bust a row with no Grace Tokens left

Hard Mode extra rule:
Each row must have at least 3 cards before it can lock at 16.
